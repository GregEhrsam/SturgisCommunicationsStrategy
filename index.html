The Sturgis Challenge

The massive influx of visitors creates an unprecedented strain on public safety infrastructure, leading to critical communication challenges that directly impact response times and officer safety.

Network Congestion

The P25 radio system faces "busy signals" during peak hours, creating dangerous communication delays for life-saving commands and coordination.

Response Delays

In dense crowds and sprawling campgrounds, pinpointing incident locations is difficult, slowing response for medical emergencies and public disturbances.

Limited Situational Awareness

Voice-only communication limits the ability of commanders to visualize incidents, track resources effectively, and make fully-informed tactical decisions.

The SmartConnect Advantage: Dynamic Offloading

APX NEXT radios with SmartConnect create a resilient hybrid system. By automatically and seamlessly routing communications over the best available path—P25, LTE, or Wi-Fi—it drastically reduces P25 congestion and extends coverage.

Next Horizon: Integrating BRINC Drone Intelligence

Through our strategic alliance with BRINC, we can integrate "Drone as a First Responder" (DFR) capabilities. This adds a critical "eye in the sky," providing unparalleled situational awareness and de-escalation options.

Rapid Incident Overwatch

Deploy a drone to an accident scene on I-90 in under 70 seconds to assess injuries and lane blockages before ground units arrive, enabling a safer, more effective response.

Crowd Analytics

Provide persistent overwatch of Main Street to monitor crowd flow and identify potential disturbances before they escalate, allowing for pre-emptive de-escalation.

One-Button Deployment

An officer pressing the emergency button on their APX NEXT can automatically trigger a drone launch to their precise GPS coordinates, providing immediate aerial support.

De-escalation & Communication

Use the drone's two-way communication to speak with subjects from a safe distance, a powerful tool for resolving high-risk situations without endangering officers.

A Tailored Solution for Every Agency

This is not a one-size-fits-all solution. The integrated ecosystem is designed to deliver specific, tangible value that addresses the unique priorities of each key stakeholder.

State Radio

Priority: System Integrity & Asset Utilization

SmartConnect dramatically reduces load on the statewide P25 system by offloading Sturgis traffic to broadband, preserving core network capacity for the rest of the state and maximizing the value of existing frequency assets.

Sheriff's Office

Priority: County-Level Command & Coordination

SmartMapping provides a county-wide common operating picture, while unified talkgroups and SmartConnect ensure seamless interoperability and reliable communication coverage across the broad jurisdiction, including rural areas.

Police Department

Priority: Rapid Response & Officer Safety

APX NEXT data suite (SmartLocate, SmartMessaging) accelerates response times and improves officer safety with real-time location tracking and discreet multimedia sharing. Drone integration provides critical overwatch for high-risk calls.
